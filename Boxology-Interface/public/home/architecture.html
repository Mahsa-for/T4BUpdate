Architecture

The Tool4Boxology Knowledge Graph (T4B KG) provides the machine-readable representation of Boxology designs created in the graphical editor. It is defined, populated, and validated through a declarative data-integration pipeline that ensures semantic consistency with the T4B Ontology and enables downstream querying, analysis, and reuse of NeSy architectures.
